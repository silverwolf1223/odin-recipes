Easy Homemade Pizza Dough

This is an easy to follow step by step recipe on how to make delicious pizza Dough in your own home.

Here are a list of the needed ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Water
  • 1 package of Dry Yeast
  • 1 pinch of White Sugar
  • 3 1/3 cups of All Purpose Flour
  • 1 tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of Chopped Basil
  1. First pour the warm water into a large bowl and dissolve the yeast and sugar
  2. Mix in the flour and olive oil
  3. turn onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth
  4. place in a well oiled bowl and let rise for 1 hour
  5. punch down and knead in garlic and basil and your pizza dough is ready!
