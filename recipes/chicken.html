Simple Baked Chicken

This recipe guides you on your journey of a healthy, easy to make meal, with one of the most versitile meats in the world.

To start off this recipe you will need

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Creole Seasoning
  • Chicken Broth

  1. To start off this recipe you're going to want to season your chicken.

    Coat your chick in olive oil and sprinkle the salt and Creole seasoning on both sides of your chicken breasts.

  2. Next bake the chicken breast at 400f for 10 to 15 minutes

  3. Finally, once you've transferred the chicken to a plate.

    Add chicken broth to the pan you used to bake then chicken and the poor it over the chicken as a sauce
