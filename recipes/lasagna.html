Lasagna

What Can Only Be Described As The World's Best Lasagna

Follow what is one of the most successful lasagna recipes in the world!

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped parsley leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
    • <1i>1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  1. cook sausage, beef, onion, and garlic in dutch oven over medium heat

  2. stir in crushed tomatos, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water

    then season with sugar, half the parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon of salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper

    simmer for about 1 1/2 hours

  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles for about 8 to 10 minutes then drain the noodles and rinse with cold water

  4. In a mixing bowl

    combine ricoota cheese with egg, remaining parsley, salt

  5. preheat oven to 375
  6. To assemble, spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 ½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese and cover with foil
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
